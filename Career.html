Engineering

Engineering is a field of work that mixes science and math. It is used to build buildings and bridges, make computers, circuits, electrical systems, and much more. The average salary is about $90,000, and its location is literally everywhere. Engineers are the cornerstone of all the infrastructure in the world.


My plan on going to engineering is first to complete Calculus AB, BC, linear algebra, and vector calculus. I also should take AP Physics 1-3, along with other science classes and get my UC requirements done so I can go to good engineering schools like Berkely or Cal Tech.
